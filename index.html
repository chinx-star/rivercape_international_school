Boarding & Accommodation

Rivercape International School offers comfortable and secure boarding house accommodation for both boys and girls. Our dormitories provide a homely environment designed to support academic focus, personal development, and well-being. Each boarding facility is supervised by experienced house parents, ensuring discipline, safety, and a nurturing community atmosphere.

  • Modern dormitory facilities with spacious rooms
  • Nutritious and balanced meals prepared daily
  • 24-hour supervision by qualified staff
  • Recreation areas for relaxation and social interaction
  • Study rooms and evening prep sessions
